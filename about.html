About Deceris LLC

Deceris is an award-winning, Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) dedicated to providing premier strategic technology and business solutions for the U.S. Federal Government. Our commitment to excellence drives us to create innovative, flexible, and impactful solutions tailored to the unique needs of our agency partners.

Partnered with minority-owned ICT leader Total Service Solutions, boasting over 20 years of success with Fortune 500 companies, our combined team offers unparalleled expertise in delivering cost-effective, cutting-edge solutions.

We prioritize building long-term relationships through open communication, outstanding client service, and a relentless focus on achieving mission success for those we serve.
